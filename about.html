Our Mission

To compound capital responsibly by turning high‑quality information into decisive action. We seek to be the rare capital manager that is both deeply fundamental and relentlessly data‑native.

What We Do

  • Machine Learning: fundamentals sentiment, earnings quality, capital allocation, competitive dynamics; integrated with systematic signals like post‑earnings drift, relative strength, and shifting global macroeconomic climate.
  • Public equities, with options overlays for risk shaping and asymmetry.

  • Event‑aware frameworks: we explicitly model earnings cycles and regime shifts, as well as more nebulous time-dependent dynamics such as seasonality.

Investment Approach

1) Idea Sourcing

  • Intelligent and grounded macroeconomic analysis.
  • Awareness the market must agree for us to be right.
  • Data pipelines for pricing, fundamentals, options, transcripts, sentiment.

2) Modeling & Validation

  • Features: pre‑earnings positioning, post‑earnings drift windows, relative strength, balance‑sheet quality.
  • Out‑of‑sample validation and stability checks to catch quietly broken features.

3) Portfolio Construction

  • Sizing from expected return and error bars; concentration with hedges over index‑like diversification.

4) Risk & Review

  • Macro, sector, idiosyncratic scenarios; pre‑/post‑mortems around earnings; strict exits on falsification.
